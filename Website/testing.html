About

Gnomes in the dark is a co-op horror/survival game where players have to navigate their way around a cave to find cogs to escape. Cogs are scattered accross a map which os filled with hidden dangers that will hunt the player down and kill them if they are not careful. Once all the cogs are collected the player is freed from the cave and the game is over
